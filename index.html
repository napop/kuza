KUZA + ShareLook partnership: Meet Esther

Esther Muia is an unemployed researcher living in Nairobi, Kenya. Last year, she joined KUZA, a cooperative that offers skills training courses. In partnership with ShareLook, KUZA trains and employs its members to develop eLearning content.

The more content is produced, the more opportunity KUZA & ShareLook can provide in training and employing Kenyans.
