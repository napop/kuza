What We Do

“ KUZA is a global development program”

KUZA is committed to creating a world where education is borderless.

KUZA is a program that brings curricula, developed by instructional designers and developers in Africa, Singapore and Bangkok, to deliver targeted skills development courses to communities where existing skills gaps in local labour markets perpetuate epidemic poverty and high unemployment rates. KUZA partners with experts, businesses, universities, NGO’s and other state and non-state actors to build a network of information. This expert information is converted into mobile learning courses, including live two-way broadcasts with experts in the field, interactive learning segments and end of course certification. KUZA shares these resources through an easy-to-use, revolutionary mobile learning app.

  • We believe the local community

    At KUZA, we believe the local community should play a participatory role in the development of the technology to ensure a culturally inclusive, applicable and effective education model and works with NGO partners, universities, businesses and communities in Africa to identify existing skills gaps and to develop courses that specifically address these learning needs.

  • We offer over 200 certified courses

    We offer over 200 certified courses developed for specific communities via an easy to use, streamlined learning mobile application, ShareLook, which combines live broadcast features, social media elements, skills training resources and a comprehensive learning management system to the communities that benefit most.
